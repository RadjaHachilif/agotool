GO-term or UniProt-keyword enrichment

GO-enrichment analyses comparing post-translationally modified proteins to unmodified proteins are likely to reveal enriched GO-terms associated with abundant proteins, rather than modified proteins specifically. We have developed a method to correct for this bias (see publication or FAQ) and this freely accessible web-tool, which you are most welcome to use.

This site can be used for functional annotation enrichment for proteomics data.
It contains tools for abundance corrected GO-term, UniProt-keyword, and KEGG pathway enrichment.
